<objective>
Build a transactional run store that makes provenance, artifact integrity, completion, locking, replacement, and deterministic checkpoint-boundary resumption explicit and fail-closed.
</objective>

<must_haves>
- Every promoted artifact has a size and SHA-256 inventory entry, and `COMPLETE` is written only after full validation.
- Completed runs are immutable by default; ordinary reruns are successful no-ops, and incompatible resumes fail with a useful diagnostic.
- Writes are temporary-file, flush, fsync, and atomic-replace operations within the destination directory.
- Locks prevent concurrent writers and stale-lock recovery is deliberate and recorded.
- Checkpoints contain model/optimizer progress, config/schema identity, RNG state, and deterministic data-order state sufficient for exact supported CPU continuation.
</must_haves>
