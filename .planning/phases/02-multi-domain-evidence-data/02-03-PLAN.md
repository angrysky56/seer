<objective>
Integrate deterministic protected partitions, duplicate/leakage audits, and separate corruption provenance into the transactional `prepare_data` publication boundary.
</objective>

<must_haves>
- Official tests remain untouched confirmatory partitions and group-related examples never cross protected partitions.
- Partition assignment is stable under row order, process, regime, and seed, using the exact research hash algorithm and integer thresholds.
- Cross-partition content/group overlap and conflicting gold fail preparation; within-partition duplicates collapse deterministically and remain counted.
- Corruptions live in separate records/artifacts and cannot enter primary natural-result iterators without an explicit capability.
- `prepare_data` publishes `dataset-lock.json`, `partition-manifest.json`, `leakage-audit.json`, assigned/deduplicated example shards, quarantine records, and separate corruption shards atomically before `COMPLETE`.
</must_haves>
